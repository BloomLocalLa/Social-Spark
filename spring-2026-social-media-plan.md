## Platform-Specific Strategies

### Facebook

**Audience:** Homeowners 35-65, local community
**Tone:** Professional, educational, community-focused

**Best Performing Content:**
- Before/after photo albums
- Educational articles/tips
- Client testimonials with photos
- Local community engagement
- Facebook Live Q&As

**Posting Times:** 9 AM, 1 PM, 6 PM CT
**Hashtags:** 3-5 local tags (#ScottLA #LafayetteLandscaping #AcadianaGardens)

**Special Tactics:**
- Boost top-performing posts ($10-25/post)
- Run lead gen ads during Phase 2
- Join local community groups (share value, not just promos)
- Respond to all comments within 2 hours

### Instagram

**Audience:** Homeowners 28-55, visual-first
**Tone:** Aspirational, beautiful, behind-the-scenes authentic

**Content Mix:**
- Feed: High-quality transformations, carousels
- Reels: Quick tips, before/afters, trending audio
- Stories: Daily BTS, polls, Q&As, job site updates

**Posting Times:** 7 AM, 12 PM, 7 PM CT
**Hashtags:** 15-20 per post, mix of:
- Local: #LafayetteLandscaping #AcadianaOutdoors #ScottLA
- Industry: #LandscapeDesign #OutdoorLiving #GardenTransformation
- Trending: #SpringVibes #CurbAppeal #BackyardGoals

**Reels Strategy:**
- Hook in first 1-2 seconds
- Text overlays for silent viewing
- Trending audio when relevant
- 15-30 seconds optimal length
- Post 4-5 reels per week minimum

**Story Highlights:**
- Transformations
- Tips & Tricks
- Testimonials
- About Us
- FAQ

### TikTok

**Audience:** Homeowners 25-45, trend-aware
**Tone:** Authentic, entertaining, educational

**Content Types:**
- Satisfying transformation videos
- Quick landscaping tips
- Trending audio adaptations
- "Day in the life" content
- ASMR-style work videos (mulch, planting, edging)
- Relatable landscaper humor

**Posting Times:** 11 AM, 3 PM, 7 PM CT
**Hashtags:** 3-5 per video
- #LandscapingTikTok #GardenTok #OutdoorTransformation
- #Louisiana #SmallBusiness #SatisfyingVideos

**TikTok-Specific Tactics:**
- Jump on trends within 48 hours
- Respond to comments with video replies
- Duet/stitch relevant content
- Use trending sounds
- Keep videos 15-60 seconds
- Strong hook in first second

**Content Series Ideas:**
1. "Things landscapers notice about your yard"
2. "Louisiana plant of the week"
3. "Client transformation reveal"
4. "Would you believe this is the same yard?"
5. "Landscaping myths busted"
